Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
Usually between 3 to 7 days, depending on the complexity of the project. Starter packages are delivered faster.
Do I need a domain name?
Yes, and we will help you purchase one. We recommend trinify.in, trinifyweb.com, trinifydigital.com, trinifystudio.com, or trinify.tech.
Are there any hidden fees?
No, our pricing is completely transparent. The initial quote is what you pay. Hosting and domain are separate, standard yearly costs.
Will my website work on mobile?
Yes, all our websites are fully responsive and look great on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
Can I update my website later?
Absolutely. We provide options for you to easily update content yourself, or we can handle ongoing maintenance for a small monthly fee.
